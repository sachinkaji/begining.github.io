how to prepare Kanda noodles

PROCEDURE :-

  1. Put noodle in an Empty Bowl, Pour A Hot water in it, stir and cover the bowl.
  2. Keep an empty pan on the stove, put oil and raw peanuts, and stir.
  3. Take another pan, put oil and cumin seeds, add mustard seeds and a pinch of asafoetida and stir.
  4. add curry leaves, chopped onions and stir.
  5. Heat for a while, add turmeric powder and chopped chillis and stir.
  6. Now add the no0ddle made from step 1, put pinch of salt and stir.
  7. add the peanuts from step2 and stir.
  8. Finally garnish with chopped coriander leaves and scroped coconut.

INGREDIENTS

  • 1 lemon
  • 3 green chillis chopped a pinch asa foetida
  • 2 table spoon coconnt scraped
  • 1 tea spoon cumin seeds
  • 4 table spoon oil
  • 1 tea spoon sugar
  • curry leaves to salt taste
